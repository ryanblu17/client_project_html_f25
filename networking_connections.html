UMSI Networking and Connections Hub

The Hub for learning to Build Meaningful Connections

The UMSI Career Development Office is a team of career development experts devoted to providing UMSI students with strategic, customized and comprehensive career services. We do much more than help students find jobs as they near graduation. We are with you every step of the way coaching, empowering and supporting you from orientation through to commencement.

Gain real-world experience

Practical experience is an essential part of our undergraduate and master’s programs. The CDO team works with students to provide them with opportunities to take what they are learning in the classroom into the real world through internships.

Tips for success in career coaching

We engage with employers locally and globally to create high touch, personalized partnerships to facilitate beneficial recruiting relationships. All employers have access to strategic consultations with our office to design a recruiting strategy customized to meet their organizational needs.

  • Prepare to be vulnerable
  • Brainstorm questions to ask or concerns you have
  • Bring any materials you want reviewed with you and be ready to share
  • Follow up on action items & schedule additional follow-up sessions (or attend drop-ins) (see instructions above)
